Biking in NC

Road biking

North Carolina is home to many road biking events everyday.

They have rides that range for 3miles to 100miles. In North Carolina they have a ride that is called the mountains to sea. The name decribes it perfectly. People ride their bikes from the mountains in Western Carolina to the Outer Banks in Eastern Carolina. This is done by a thing called bikepacking. That is where you ride from campsite to campsite each day.
